# Demo Script: AI Digital Shadow Tracker

This script outlines a 5-minute demonstration of the full-stack capabilities.

## 🕒 Phase 1: The Problem (0:00 - 1:00)
- **Action**: Open the App on the Login Screen.
- **Narrative**: "In the age of AI, our digital footprint is no longer just text. Our faces and personal data are being scraped and misused in scams, fake profiles, and deepfakes. How do you know where your data is right now?"

## 🕒 Phase 2: The Action (1:00 - 2:00)
- **Action**: Log in and navigate to the 'New Scan' screen. Select `datasets/images/original_profile.png`.
- **Narrative**: "Meet our user, Rahul. He's concerned about his professional photo. He uploads it to the Shadow Tracker. Behind the scenes, the image is securely stored in Firebase, and a Cloud Function has already woken up the AI Engine."

## 🕒 Phase 3: The Intelligence (2:00 - 3:30)
- **Action**: Switch to the Dashboard and show the new entry. Click on it to see details.
- **Narrative**: "Within seconds, our Python AI Engine has generated a visual signature of Rahul's face. It searched the web and found a match. But it didn't just stop at similarity. Using **Google Gemini**, the system analyzed the context. It discovered that this photo is being used on a crypto-scam site under a fake name."

## 🕒 Phase 4: The Alert (3:30 - 4:30)
- **Action**: Navigate to the Alerts screen. Show the red 'High Risk' alert.
- **Narrative**: "Because the risk score was 85%, the system automatically escalated this to a High-Risk Alert. Rahul receives a push notification immediately, allowing him to take action before damage is done."

## 🕒 Phase 5: The Impact (4:30 - 5:00)
- **Action**: Return to the Dashboard overview.
- **Narrative**: "The AI Digital Shadow Tracker doesn't just find matches; it provides peace of mind through automated, intelligent monitoring. Your digital shadow, now under your control."
